About me

Ahanaf Tahmid is a passionate structural engineer dedicated to research, innovation, and sustainable design. With a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering and a robust academic and professional foundation, he has contributed significantly to advancing engineering knowledge. His research spans advanced materials, concrete technologies, FRPs, fiber composites, and self-healing materials, culminating in the publication of nearly 17 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences. Currently pursuing a Master of Applied Science in Engineering, specializing in Wood and Timber Engineering at the University of Northern British Columbia (UNBC), Ahanaf serves as a Research Assistant under the guidance of Professor Thomas Tannert.
